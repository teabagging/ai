:The evolution of chemical defense mechanisms in plants is a dynamic process shaped by interactions with herbivores, environmental pressures, and genetic adaptability. Types of Chemical Defenses and Their Targets - Alkaloids (e.g., nicotine, caffeine): Interfere with nervous systems, effective against insects.
