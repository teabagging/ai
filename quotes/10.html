ApproachThe key insight to solve this problem is to recognize that isomorphic strings must have the same structure in terms of character occurrences. This means that the pattern of indices of the first occurrence of each character in both strings must be identical. Compare Patterns: If the normalized patterns of both strings are identical, the strings are isomorphic; otherwise, they are not.
